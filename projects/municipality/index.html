Our Municipality
Socio-economically, the Municipality of Lamia oscillates between the urban character of the city of Lamia with characteristics of a developing regional city and a commercial-industrial pole at a junction of the road "Athens - Thessaloniki" and the declining agricultural character of the Delta and Sperch Delta. other rural areas of the Municipality of Lamia. Historical-archaeological research highlights the long history of Lamia and other areas of the Municipality as cities with a special administrative or commercial and communication role.
