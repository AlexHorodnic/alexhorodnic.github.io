FOOTBALL TOURNAMENT LEAGUE 11/04
The Algarve Cup is an invitational tournament for national teams in men's association football hosted by the Portuguese Football Federation (FPF). Held annually in the Algarve region of Portugal since 1994, it is one of the most prestigious and longest-running women's international football events.

CHESS TOURNAMENT 10/02
In the Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC) Season 20 Superfinal the top two engines from the Premier Division compete in a 100-game match for the TCEC Grand Champion title. In the case of a drawn match there will be a rapid match of 16 games with a time control of 25' + 10" with random openings selected from earlier in the same Season.
